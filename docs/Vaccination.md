# Vaccination

NOTE: NEVER vaccinate sick chicken.

Vaccines are preparations that protect chicken from infectious diseases but they cannot treat diseases. Vaccines protect chicken against speci?c diseases. Vaccines work best on healthy, well-managed chicken.

## Importance of Vaccination

1. Infectious disease causing organism are always present in the environment and are dificult to control

2. To reduce production losses due to poor health and death caused by diseases, to protect chicken against diseases causing egg production drop and egg shell deformities

## Vaccine Specificity

Chicken may suffer from other diseases despite successful vaccination for another disease. Vaccination can never
provide 100% protection.


## Rules of Vaccination

1. Vaccination should not be administered to sick birds
2. Follow vaccination guideline from the breed supplier
3. Cost the benefits of vaccination against potential loss
4. Maternal antibody status may affect effectiveness of the vaccine
5. Vaccines should be stored at 4 - 8Â°C and protected from heat and direct sunlight.
6. Vaccines should be transported in a cool box,
7. All equipment used for vaccination should be washed with clean water.
